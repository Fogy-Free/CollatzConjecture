Collatz Conjecture

Collatz conjecture in mathematics states that if you start with any positive integer and repeatedly apply the following two rules, you will eventually reach 1: If the number is even, divide it by 2. If the number is odd, multiply it by 3 and add 1. As you can see, the sequence eventually reaches 1, no matter what the starting number is. The Collatz conjecture states that this will always happen, no matter what positive integer you start with. The Collatz conjecture is one of the most famous unsolved problems in mathematics. It has been studied by mathematicians for over a century, but no one has been able to prove or disprove it. Also, it is the math equation on the students door in Wakanda forever.
